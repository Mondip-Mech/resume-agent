Senior Data Scientist — Acme Analytics

About the Role
We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our growing ML platform team.
You will build, train, and deploy machine learning models that power our core product.

Required Skills
- 5+ years of Python experience
- SQL and BigQuery for data analysis and pipeline work
- Machine Learning frameworks: TensorFlow or PyTorch
- Experience deploying models on AWS (S3, EC2, SageMaker)
- A/B Testing and statistical experimentation
- MLOps: model versioning, monitoring, and retraining pipelines

Preferred Skills
- Docker and Kubernetes for containerized deployments
- Apache Spark or Airflow for large-scale ETL pipelines
- Tableau or Looker for stakeholder dashboards
- Experience with LLMs and RAG systems

Responsibilities
- Build and deploy ML models to production serving millions of users
- Design scalable data pipelines processing terabytes of data daily
- Collaborate with engineering and product teams on model integration
- Mentor junior data scientists and conduct code reviews
- Present findings to non-technical stakeholders

Our Culture
Fast-paced, data-driven, collaborative, high ownership. We move quickly and
expect candidates to take initiative and drive outcomes end-to-end.

Compensation: $160,000 – $200,000 + equity
Location: Remote-first (US only)
